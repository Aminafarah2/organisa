What We Do

Our organization is involved in various initiatives aimed at supporting the poor and underprivileged. We came up with projects that help the society.

Support the orphans with basic needs

Our team recently had the privilege of visiting Good Hope Markazil Banatil Islamia children's home to distribute food and essential supplies. Witnessing the joy and gratitude on the children's faces was truly heartwarming.

We are grateful for the opportunity to make a positive impact on their lives and support their well-being. Your contribution helps us continue these vital initiatives.We were overwhelmed by the warmth and resilience of the children, despite the challenges they face. We distributed essential supplies, including food, clothing, and hygiene products, and spent time playing games and interacting with the children. The joy and gratitude in their eyes were truly inspiring. It's a privilege to be able to support such a wonderful organization and make a positive difference in these children's lives.

Well Construction

In collaboration with Holding Hands Organization, our team successfully completed the construction of a well at Ummu Asia children's home. This new water source will significantly enhance the health and well-being of the children residing there.

The well provides clean water to the children, significantly improving their daily lives and health. Your support in this project ensures they have access to this essential resource,"Together with Holding Hands Organization, we have invested in the future of the children at Ummu Asia children's home. The well we installed will provide a sustainable source of clean water for years to come, ensuring that these children have the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential.

Food Distribution

We organize food distribution drives to combat hunger and ensure that everyone has access to nutritious meals.Your contributions help us continue providing these essential food supplies to those in need, ensuring they have the nutrition required to thrive.

For many children in orphanages, these gifts may be the only ones they receive, making the gesture even more significant. It not only brings immediate happiness but also fosters a sense of belonging and inclusion. The joy that these children experience during Eid when they receive gifts is a powerful reminder of the impact that small acts of kindness can have on the lives of others. Organizations and individuals who participate in gift-giving during Eid help create memorable experiences for these children, reinforcing the values of empathy and generosity that are central to the holiday. It's a beautiful way to spread love and happiness, ensuring that every child, regardless of their circumstances, can celebrate Eid with a smile.

Enhancement of Mosque

Our latest project involves the construction of a mosque in a rural area, providing the community with a place for prayer and gathering. This mosque will serve as a center for spiritual and social activities, fostering a sense of unity and belonging among the residents.

"Our community has taken a significant step towards enhancing the spiritual experience of our congregation. By investing in new, high-quality speakers, we have significantly improved the sound quality during prayers and sermons. These upgraded speakers ensure that everyone can hear clearly and fully participate in religious activities. This enhancement fosters a more inclusive and fulfilling worship experience for all."
